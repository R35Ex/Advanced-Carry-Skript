 # 🌟 AdvancedCarry Premium | The Ultimate Transport Solution

Say goodbye to annoying boats, tedious minecarts, and moving items back and forth! **AdvancedCarry** is the ultimate lightweight Skript that allows your players to literally carry **Mobs, Containers, and Decorative Blocks** on their heads.

Whether it's relocating a full Chest of diamonds, stealing a Villager for a trading hall, or moving an Anvil without breaking it—AdvancedCarry makes it flawless and fun!

## 🚀 Premium Features

- 🐄 **Carry ANY Mob**: Shift + Right Click to carry animals and passive mobs (Villagers, Cows, Turtles, Pandas, etc.). No more boats needed!
- 📦 **Carry Full Containers**: Shift + Left Click to pick up Chests, Barrels, and Trapped Chests. The entire inventory inside is securely saved!
- 🧱 **Carry Decorative Blocks**: Move Anvils, Bookshelves, Glass, or even Enchanting Table safely without needing a Silk Touch pickaxe.
- ⚙️ **100% Customizable**: Want to allow carrying specific custom blocks or even iron golems? You can! The configuration is incredibly easy—just add or remove block/mob names directly at the very top of the script.
- 🛡️ **Bulletproof Anti-Dupe & Safety**: What happens if a player disconnects, crashes, or dies while carrying a chest full of diamonds? **Nothing is lost!** The script actively tracks the carried object and forces it to safely place itself at the player's last location. Zero dupes, zero ghost blocks!
- ⚡ **Zero Lag & TPS Friendly**: Unlike older carry plugins that use heavy teleportation loops, AdvancedCarry uses Minecraft's native `Passenger` and `Block Display` systems. It's client-side rendered, meaning absolutely 0 server strain even if 500 players carry chests at once.
- 🔒 **Claim Protection Support**: Fully respects region protections (WorldGuard, GriefPrevention, Lands, etc.). Players cannot steal blocks or kidnap mobs from someone else's claimed land!

## 📖 How to Use
Players can type `/carry help` to view the beautiful instruction menu at any time:
- **Carrying Blocks/Containers**: Empty hand + Sneak (Shift) + Left Click.
- **Carrying Mobs**: Empty hand + Sneak (Shift) + Right Click.
- **Placing Down**: Look at the ground and Sneak (Shift) + Right Click.

### Commands:
- `/carry help` - Shows the interactive instruction menu.
- `/carry reset` - Safely drops the carried item and resets the player's state in case of any visual glitches.

## ⚙️ Requirements
- Minecraft 1.19.4 - 1.21+ (Requires native Block Display support).
- [Skript](https://github.com/SkriptLang/Skript) (Tested on the latest versions).

## 📥 Installation & Setup
  1. Download the `advancedCarry.sk` file.
  2. Place it into your server's `plugins/Skript/scripts/` folder.
  3. Open the file to easily edit the Allowed Blocks & Mobs list at the top.
  4. Type `/sk reload advancedCarry` in your console or in-game.
  5. Enjoy the ultimate premium feature on your server!
